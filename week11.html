Representing Natural Forces

Here is my project. I created a scene with snow falling and a snowman. There are two sliders. One controls the rate of snow fall, and the second controls the wind speed. I initially thought about creating a temperature slider that effected the whole scene, so when it was higher than freezing, the scene would "melt" and when sliding back over to below freezing, snow would fall again.
